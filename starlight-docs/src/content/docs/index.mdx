## Welcome to OpenSearch AgentOps

OpenSearch AgentOps is a comprehensive platform for observing, evaluating, and optimizing your AI applications. This guide will help you get started quickly.

## What You Can Do

### Instrument Your AI Applications
Add tracing and monitoring to your AI applications with minimal code changes. Support for OpenTelemetry, custom tracing, and popular AI frameworks.

### Observe in Real-Time
View traces, sessions, and agent behavior in real-time. Track tool calls, reasoning steps, and multi-modal interactions.

### Evaluate Performance
Create datasets, run experiments, and compare results. Integrate evaluations into your CI/CD pipeline.

### Manage Prompts
Store, version, and optimize your prompts. Deploy prompts across environments with confidence.
